Guitars are so Cool

09 December 2008

I haven't played my guitar in quite a while. I don't know what happened, I just kind of lost interest in it. Sometimes I pick it up and play some stuff but otherwise it just sits in the corner of my room collecting dust.

I don't really remember when I first started playing. I think it was just random thing I wanted to learn to do. So I went online and bought one. I remember the first day I got it, I played with it almost the whole day. It wasn't any good. I never really practiced much either. I'd go online and download some tabs and learn my favorite parts to songs that I've been listening to. It was a good way to waste time.

Sometimes I would go to my friends house and we would play. We really sucked. We tried starting a band but that was an epic fail. We didn't have a drummer. When we did find one and finally had a band meet, I wasn't there. And after that the band never met up again. That was in junior high. We all went to different high schools and I moved to Brooklyn.

I haven't really played after that. Sometimes I hear a song in a movie or something, look up the tabs and try to play it but end up just giving up cause its too hard.

I love watching people jam. It's so cool. When I see them I think to myself, "Shit, if I had practiced a crap load more I'd be as awesome as them". Shit, guitars are so cool.
