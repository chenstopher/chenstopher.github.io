Snowboarding at Windham

31 December 2011

I went snowboarding at Windham Mountain for a couple of days during Christmas. There wasn't much snowfall this year so not many of the trails were open but it was better that I expected. They have this waffle house thing near the base of the hill so every time you get on the lift you smelled awesomeness. I thought that was pretty awesome.

Finally learned how to ride switch. The trick is toe-side turns = squeeze your knees together, heel-side turns = push your knees apart.

I brought my Drift camera with me and a got a bunch of videos. The one above is from one of the night sessions runs. It's my favorite video cause Georo falls a lot in it. And by a lot I mean every time he tries to do a jump.
