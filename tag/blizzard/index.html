Blizzard 12262010

26 December 2010

It is dumping snow outside.

I tried to build a snow fort today but I got bored after building one wall.

Then Cliff decided to turn the stairs leading out the the backyard into a ramp so he could snowboard off it, and he did.

Then he got tired and I buried him in snow and Chloe (our dog) almost peed on him.

Finally, we went back inside and I tried to find a good movie to instant watch on Netflix but I realized I’ve seen all of good ones already.

These seem like good captions for photos. Unfortunately, I don’t take any.

I can’t wait for Wednesday.
