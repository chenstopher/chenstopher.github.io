Messenger Bags are Pretty Cool

01 November 2008

So here's my old messenger bag. I found it on dell.com and thought it was the coolest messenger bag in the world. It was cheap, had some awesome tattoo-like thing on it, and some super innovative closing mechanism. So I ordered it and it came. Shit, I was wrong. It was a bigger than I had expected. The tattoo-like thing looked kinda ghey. And the innovative closing mechanism took for-freaking-ever to close! If I packed it with my laptop and other things, it got really heavy and my shoulder would hurt cause there is no padding on the strap. But whatever, I didn't feel like getting another one so I dealt with that crap for a couple months.

Then I found Chrome bags. Man, they are so awesome. They look pretty awesome and they have this super cool seatbelt strap thing. I did some research and found out they are pretty much indestructible. One thing I was worried about was size cause all the pics on the site made the bag look HUGE. But whatever, I ordered it anyway. I got the smallest size, cause I didn't want it to be too big.

After about a week it came. And here it is. Looks pretty awesome. Got the Red/black stripe one cause red is awesome and so is black. Check out that SUPER COOL seatbelt buckle! The strap is also really comfortable and easily adjustable.

One problem though. ITS TOO FREAKING SMALL! I put my laptop, laptop charger, a couple magazines and a chemistry workbook from sophomore year of high school that I found in the bag and it barely fit. The bag looks like its gonna explode.

I emailed Chrome. I'm gonna exchange it for the next size up. Hopefully it will be the right size. Shipping is expensive!

I hate it when I get hyped up for something I ordered online and when it finally gets here it turns out not how I expected. Sigh.
