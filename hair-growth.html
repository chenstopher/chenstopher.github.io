Hair Growth

15 November 2010

Wouldn’t it be interesting if hair grew up to a certain length and then stopped? Imagine how our lives would be different. Would social status would be based on the length of your hair? Would barbers exist? Would we now have a new form of prejudice called hairism? What kind of interesting hair products would be created from this? Would the average male hair length be shorter or longer than female hair length?

This is the type of stuff I think about when I can’t sleep.
